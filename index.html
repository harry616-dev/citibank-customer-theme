Frequently Asked Questions

How can I track my order?

Once your order is shipped, you will receive a tracking number via email. You can use this number to track your package on our website or the carrier's website. Orders typically take 3-7 business days to arrive.

What is your return policy?

We accept returns within 30 days of purchase. Items must be in original condition with tags attached. Return shipping is free for defective items, while customer returns may incur a small shipping fee.

How do I cancel my order?

Orders can be cancelled within 24 hours of placement. After this time, the order may have already been processed for shipping. Please check your order status in your account dashboard.

Do you offer international shipping?

Yes, we ship to most countries worldwide. International shipping costs and delivery times vary by location. Additional customs fees may apply depending on your country's regulations.

How can I update my account information?

You can update your account information by logging into your account dashboard. From there, you can change your address, payment methods, and personal details.

Return & Exchange Process

Check Eligibility: Ensure your item is within the 30-day return window and meets our return conditions.
Initiate Return: Log into your account and select the order you wish to return. Choose the items and reason for return.
Print Return Label: Download and print the prepaid return shipping label provided.
Package Items: Securely package the items in their original packaging with all tags and accessories.
Ship Back: Attach the return label and drop off at any authorized shipping location.
Receive Refund: Once we receive and inspect your return, your refund will be processed within 5-7 business days.

Order Issues & Resolution

Damaged or Defective Items

If you receive a damaged or defective item, please report any issues within 7 days of receiving your order. We'll need your order number and photos of the damaged item to process your replacement quickly.

Wrong Item or Missing Items

If you received the wrong item or have missing items, we'll send you the correct items and provide a prepaid return label if needed. Please check your order confirmation email first to verify what was ordered.

Account & Payment Support

Account Access Problems

If you're having trouble accessing your account, try resetting your password using the "Forgot Password" link. Make sure you're using the correct email address associated with your account.

Payment Issues

We accept all major credit cards, PayPal, and digital wallets. If your payment is declined, please check with your bank. Ensure your billing address matches the address on file with your bank.
